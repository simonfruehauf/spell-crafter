## The Arena âš”ï¸

Combat is automatic and idle-based:

| Stat | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| `HP` | Your health points. Regenerates between fights. |
| `ARC` | Arcane Power. Increases spell damage. |
| `VIT` | Vitality. Improves HP regeneration. |
| `BAR` | Barrier. Reduces incoming damage. |

**Combat Flow:**
1. Your spells cast automatically against the current enemy
2. Defeat enemies for Gold, Experience, and Crafting Resources
3. Use resources in the Workshop and Armory

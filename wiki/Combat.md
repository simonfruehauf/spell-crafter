## Mechanics

### Damage Types
*   **Physical**: Standard damage, mitigated by Barrier (BAR).
*   **Fire**: Effective against Beasts and Nature. Burns targets.
*   **Ice**: Effective against Fire and Flying. Slows targets.
*   **Lightning**: Effective against Water and Constructs. Chains between targets.
*   **Earth**: Effective against Lightning. Provides defensive bonuses.
*   **Dark**: Effective against Holy and Living. Drains life.
*   **Holy**: Effective against Undead and Dark. Heals and protects.
*   **Arcane**: Pure magic. Effective against heavy armor.

# About

> *An experimental vibecoded project where magic meets incremental gameplay.*

**Spell Crafter** is an incremental RPG about mastering magic. Begin as a humble apprentice, delve into arcane research, craft powerful spells from ancient runes, and battle creatures from goblins to cosmic horrors.

## Spell Crafting

In **The Scriptorium**, combine runes to create powerful spells.

| Concept | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Rune Slots** | Each spell can hold 1-10 runes (base: 3, upgradeable) |
| **Mana Cost** | Total of all rune mana costs (reduced by upgrades) |
| **Spell Mastery** | Spells gain experience and level up with use |
| **Binding** | Once bound, spells are added to your **Grimoire** |
